The Freelegal platform allows clients to submit pro bono cases through two distinct submission pathways: identified submissions with full contact information, or anonymous submissions for privacy-conscious clients.

## Submission types

Clients can toggle between two submission modes based on their privacy preferences:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Identified submission">
    The standard submission form requires full contact information, allowing for direct communication and case tracking. This is recommended for clients who want to stay updated on their case status.

    ### Form fields

    All fields marked with an asterisk (*) are required:

    - **Case Title*** - Brief title describing the legal issue
    - **Legal Category*** - Select from predefined categories
    - **Case Description*** - Detailed description including relevant facts, timelines, and the type of help needed
    - **Contact Name*** - Full name of the person submitting
    - **Phone Number*** - Contact phone number (format: +233-000-000)
    - **Email*** - Email address for correspondence

    <Note>
      Your information is kept confidential and will only be shared with approved legal representatives and account managers.
    </Note>

    ### Switching to anonymous

    If you're concerned about privacy, you can switch to anonymous submission at any time by clicking the "Make an anonymous submission" button in the yellow privacy banner.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Anonymous submission">
    Anonymous submissions allow clients to submit cases without providing personal contact information. This option is ideal for sensitive situations where privacy is paramount.

    ### Form fields

    Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*):

    - **Case Title*** - Brief title describing the legal issue
    - **Legal Category*** - Select from predefined categories
    - **Case Description*** - Detailed description including relevant facts, timelines, and the type of help needed
    - **Contact Name** (optional) - Can be left blank
    - **Phone Number** (optional) - Can be left blank
    - **Email** (optional) - Can be left blank

    <Warning>
      Anonymous submission requires acknowledging that limited contact information may restrict follow-up capabilities. You must check the acknowledgment box to submit.
    </Warning>

    ### Switching to identified

    If you want to track your case and receive updates, you can switch to identified submission by clicking "Make an identified submission" in the yellow banner.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Submission process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose submission type">
    Decide whether you want to submit with your contact information (identified) or without it (anonymous).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill out the form">
    Complete all required fields marked with a red asterisk. Provide as much detail as possible in the case description.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review privacy information">
    Review the confidentiality notice at the bottom of the form. Your information is only shared with approved legal professionals.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your case">
    Click the "Submit Case" button. For anonymous submissions, you must first check the acknowledgment box.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive confirmation">
    After successful submission, you'll receive a confirmation modal with your unique Case ID. Save this ID for future reference.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## After submission

Once your case is submitted:

- You'll receive a unique Case ID (e.g., 2BO98XC for identified or 239DN09 for anonymous submissions)
- Account managers review your case and match it with appropriate legal representatives
- If you provided contact information, you'll be notified when a firm expresses interest
- The case enters the assignment workflow for review by law firms

<Note>
  Keep your Case ID in a safe place. You'll need it to reference your case if you contact the platform.
</Note>

## Privacy and confidentiality

All submissions are handled with strict confidentiality:

- Information is only shared with approved legal representatives and account managers
- Anonymous submissions provide an additional layer of privacy protection
- All data is stored securely and not shared with third parties
- You can provide partial contact information in anonymous mode if you want some follow-up capability

<Warning>
  While anonymous submission protects your identity, it may limit the ability of legal representatives to contact you for clarifications or updates. Consider providing at least an email address if possible.
</Warning>
