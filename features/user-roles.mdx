The Freelegal platform supports three distinct user roles, each with specific responsibilities and access levels in the pro bono legal services workflow.

## Overview of roles

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Clients" icon="user">
    Submit legal cases and track their status through the platform
  </Card>
  <Card title="Account managers" icon="user-graduate">
    Law students who review cases and assign them to appropriate firms
  </Card>
  <Card title="Legal representatives" icon="briefcase">
    Lawyers and law firms who browse cases, express interest, and provide representation
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Clients

Clients are individuals seeking pro bono legal representation. They can access the platform without creating an account.

### Capabilities

- Submit cases through identified or anonymous forms
- Receive a unique Case ID for tracking purposes
- Describe legal situations in detail
- Select appropriate legal categories
- Choose their level of privacy (identified vs anonymous)

### Access

Clients do not need to register for an account. They can directly access the case submission form and submit their legal matters.

<Note>
  Clients who submit identified cases with contact information will receive updates on their case status through the provided email or phone number.
</Note>

## Account managers

Account managers are typically law students who volunteer to help triage and assign cases. They serve as the bridge between clients and legal representatives.

### Capabilities

- Review all submitted cases
- Assign cases to appropriate law firms
- Monitor case status and progression
- View the leaderboard to track firm engagement
- Manage case assignments and withdrawals

### Dashboard access

Account managers have access to:

- **Available cases tab** - View all unassigned cases
- **Assigned cases tab** - Track cases already assigned to firms
- **Case details** - View full case information including descriptions and categories
- **Leaderboard** - Monitor firm participation and recognize top contributors

### Registration process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to registration">
    Go to the registration page and select "Law student / Account manager" as your user type.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Provide personal information">
    Fill in:
    - Full Name (required)
    - Email (required)
    - Password (required)
    - Confirm Password (required)
    - School Name (optional)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create account">
    Click "Register" to create your account. You'll be redirected to the manager dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Legal representatives

Legal representatives include individual lawyers and law firms who provide pro bono services through the platform.

### Capabilities

- Browse all available pro bono cases
- Express interest in cases that match their expertise
- View cases where they've expressed interest
- Manage case assignments
- Withdraw interest if unable to take a case
- Earn recognition points for participation

### Dashboard access

Legal representatives have access to:

- **Available cases tab** - Browse all cases available for representation
- **Interested cases tab** - View cases where interest has been expressed
- **Case actions** - View full details and express/withdraw interest
- **Case filtering** - See cases by status (New, In Progress)

### Registration process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select user type">
    On the registration page, choose "Legal Representative" as your user type.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter basic information">
    Provide:
    - Full Name (required)
    - Email (required)
    - Password (required)
    - Confirm Password (required)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose representation type">
    Select whether you're registering as:
    - **Firm** - Representing a law firm (requires Firm Name)
    - **Individual** - Solo practitioner or independent lawyer
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete registration">
    If you selected "Firm", enter your Firm Name (e.g., "Pearson Hardmann"). Then click "Register" to create your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Firm names are displayed on the leaderboard when your organization earns recognition points for pro bono work.
</Note>

## Role-specific workflows

### Account manager workflow

1. Log in to the manager dashboard
2. Review available cases in the "Available" tab
3. Click "View" to see full case details
4. Click "Assign" to assign the case to a firm
5. Monitor assigned cases in the "Assigned" tab
6. Track firm participation through the leaderboard

### Legal representative workflow

1. Log in to the firm dashboard
2. Browse available cases in the "Available Cases" tab
3. Click "View" to read case details
4. Click "Express Interest" to indicate willingness to take the case
5. Monitor interested cases in the "My Interested Cases" tab
6. Wait for account manager assignment confirmation

<Warning>
  Only approved legal representatives and account managers have access to client contact information. All users must maintain strict confidentiality of case details.
</Warning>

## Switching between roles

If you need to access the platform with a different role, you must create a separate account for each role. This ensures proper access controls and maintains the integrity of the case management workflow.

<Note>
  Already have an account? Use the "Login" link on the registration page to access your existing account instead of creating a new one.
</Note>
