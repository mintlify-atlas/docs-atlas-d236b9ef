## Who we serve

Our platform brings together three key groups to widen access to justice:

### Clients in need

If you're facing a legal issue and need support, Freelegal provides a simple way to submit your case and connect with legal professionals who can help. You can submit cases with full contact details or anonymously if privacy is a concern.

### Law students and account managers

Law student volunteers serve as account managers who review submitted cases, prepare case documentation, and assign cases to legal representatives. This provides valuable real-world experience while helping those in need.

### Legal representatives and law firms

Legal professionals and firms can browse available pro bono cases, express interest in cases that match their expertise, and provide representation. The platform includes a leaderboard system that recognizes firms for their pro bono contributions.

## Key features

<Note>
  All information shared on the platform is kept confidential and only accessible to approved users in their respective roles.
</Note>

### Pro bono case submission

Clients can submit legal cases through an intuitive form that captures:
- Case title and legal category (Abuse, Family Law, Immigration, Housing/Tenancy, Employment, Consumer Rights, Criminal Defense, etc.)
- Detailed case description
- Contact information (optional for anonymous submissions)

### Anonymous submission option

Privacy-conscious clients can submit cases without providing personal contact information. The platform offers flexibility to switch between anonymous and identified submissions.

### Multi-role user system

The platform supports three distinct user types:
- **Clients**: Submit cases and track their status
- **Law Students/Account Managers**: Review, manage, and assign cases to legal representatives
- **Legal Representatives**: Browse cases, express interest, and provide pro bono services

### Case management with status tracking

Cases move through clearly defined statuses:
- **New**: Recently submitted and awaiting review
- **In Progress**: Active cases being worked on by legal representatives

Both account managers and legal representatives have dedicated dashboards to track and manage cases efficiently.

### Gamification through leaderboard

The platform includes a competitive leaderboard system to recognize and encourage pro bono work:
- **+100 points**: Successfully completing a pro bono case
- **+25 points**: Expressing interest in a case
- **+50 points**: Each month of ongoing representation
- **+10 points**: Referring a case to another lawyer when unable to take it

<Tip>
  Legal representatives can track their firm's ranking and see how they compare with other firms contributing pro bono services.
</Tip>
