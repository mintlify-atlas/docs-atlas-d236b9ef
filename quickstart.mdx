# Getting started with Freelegal

This guide will help you get started with Freelegal based on your role in the platform. Whether you're seeking legal help, managing cases as a law student, or providing pro bono services as a legal representative, we've got you covered.

## For clients

If you need legal assistance, Freelegal makes it easy to submit your case and connect with pro bono legal services.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the case submission page">
    Visit the Freelegal homepage and click the "Submit Case" button on the hero section, or navigate directly to `/submit`.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose your submission type">
    Decide whether you want to submit your case with full contact information or anonymously.
    
    **Identified submission**: Includes your name, phone number, and email so legal professionals can contact you directly.
    
    **Anonymous submission**: Submit case details without personal information. You can optionally include contact details later if needed.
    
    <Tip>
      You can switch between submission types using the toggle buttons on the form.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Fill out the case details">
    Provide the following information:
    - **Case Title**: A brief title describing your legal issue
    - **Legal Category**: Select from categories including:
      - Abuse
      - Family Law
      - Immigration
      - Housing/Tenancy
      - Employment
      - Consumer Rights
      - Criminal Defense
      - Other
    - **Case Description**: Detailed explanation of your legal situation, including relevant facts, timelines, and the type of help you're seeking
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add contact information (if not submitting anonymously)">
    For identified submissions, provide:
    - Full name
    - Phone number
    - Email address
    
    <Note>
      For anonymous submissions, contact information is optional. Note that submitting anonymously may limit the ability to follow up unless you provide contact info.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit your case">
    Review your information and click "Submit Case". You'll receive a case ID that you can use to reference your submission.
    
    Your information is kept confidential and will only be shared with approved legal representatives and account managers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## For account managers

Law students and account managers play a crucial role in reviewing cases and connecting them with legal representatives.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Navigate to `/register` and select "Law student / Account manager" as your registration type.
    
    Fill in the required information:
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Password
    - School name (optional)
    
    Click "Register" to create your account.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log in to your account">
    After registration, navigate to `/login` and enter your credentials to access your account manager dashboard.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access your dashboard">
    Once logged in, you'll be directed to the Case Portal at `/managerDash`. The dashboard displays:
    - Available pro bono cases awaiting assignment
    - Cases you've already assigned to legal representatives
    - The leaderboard showing top-performing firms
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review available cases">
    Browse the "Available" tab to see new cases submitted by clients. Each case displays:
    - Case ID
    - Title and category
    - Submission date
    - Current status (New or In Progress)
    
    Click "View" to see full case details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Assign cases to legal representatives">
    When you identify a suitable case for a legal representative:
    1. Click the "Assign" button next to the case
    2. The case will be assigned to a legal representative
    3. Track assigned cases in the "Assigned" tab
    
    You can withdraw assignments if needed using the "Withdraw assignment" button.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor the leaderboard">
    View the leaderboard section to see which firms are leading in pro bono contributions. This helps you understand which firms are most active and may be good candidates for future case assignments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Account managers serve as the bridge between clients and legal representatives, ensuring cases are properly reviewed and matched with appropriate legal expertise.
</Note>

## For legal representatives

Legal professionals and law firms can browse cases, express interest, and provide pro bono services through Freelegal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register as a legal representative">
    Navigate to `/register` and select "Legal Representative" as your registration type.
    
    Provide the required information:
    - Full name
    - Email address
    - Password
    - Choose whether you're registering as part of a firm or as an individual
    - If part of a firm, enter your firm name (e.g., "Pearson Hardmann")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Log in to access your dashboard">
    After registration, log in at `/login` using your credentials. You'll be directed to the Legal Representative Case Portal.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse available cases">
    Your dashboard at `/firmDashboard` displays two tabs:
    - **Available Cases**: New pro bono cases you can express interest in
    - **My Interested Cases**: Cases where you've already expressed interest
    
    Each case listing shows:
    - Case ID and title
    - Legal category
    - Date submitted
    - Current status
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Express interest in cases">
    When you find a case that matches your expertise:
    1. Click "View" to see full case details
    2. If you want to take the case, click "Express Interest"
    3. The case will move to your "My Interested Cases" tab
    
    <Tip>
      Expressing interest in a case earns you **+25 points** on the leaderboard!
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage your cases">
    Track all cases you're interested in through the "My Interested Cases" tab. You can:
    - View case details at any time
    - Withdraw interest if you're unable to take the case
    - Update case status as you progress
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Earn recognition">
    As you complete pro bono work, you'll earn points:
    - **+100 points**: Successfully completing a pro bono case
    - **+25 points**: Expressing interest in a case
    - **+50 points**: Each month of ongoing representation
    - **+10 points**: Referring a case to another lawyer
    
    Your firm's ranking will appear on the leaderboard visible to all users.
  </Step>
</Steps>
